welcome
This section contains 2 training splits. Training splits are ways to organise your workout efficiently and optimally which, along with proper determination and diet, will give results. Even with a split that works best for you, its important to keep in mind that whatever exercise you do, executing it with proper form and technique is the most crucial thing in order to properly track your progress over time Take 3-4 minutes of rest between each set, and warm up with lighter sets before performing your working sets if need be. Take your sets close to failure(0 RIR) without compromising the form and aim to progressively overload each session, whether it be small increments in weights or reps.
