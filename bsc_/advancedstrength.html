This section contains 1 training split. Training splits are ways to organise your workout efficiently and optimally which, along with proper determination and diet, will give results. Even with a split that works best for you, its important to keep in mind that whatever exercise you do, executing it with proper form and technique is the most crucial thing in order to properly track your progress over time Take 5 minutes of rest between your main compound sets, and 2-3 minute rest between your isolation sets, and warm up with lighter sets before performing your working sets if need be. Take your sets close to 2-3 RIR for compounds and 0-1 RIR for isolation without compromising the form and aim to progressively overload each session, whether it be small increments in weights or reps.
3 Day Split
This is a 3 day split,1 day for each of the big 3 lift, ideal for those who can not go to the gym for more than 3 days a week. Each training day is followed by a rest day(dont ignore rest days).
